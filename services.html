Our Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga

Gentle Hatha Yoga focuses on slower-paced movements, ideal for beginners or those looking to unwind. It involves gentle stretching and mindful breathing, promoting relaxation and flexibility.

Vinyasa Yoga

    Vinyasa Yoga is a dynamic style of yoga, where movements are linked with breath. Each pose flows seamlessly into the next, offering a more intense practice that builds strength, endurance, and mindfulness.

Advanced Yoga

For those looking to challenge themselves, Advanced Yoga includes more complex poses, focusing on balance, strength, and flexibility. This class is ideal for experienced practitioners.
